\subsection{Running gear}
\frame{\frametitle{Requirements on running gear}
\framesubtitle{}
\begin{itemize}
\item Transfer of vertical forces between vehicle and rail
\item Transfer of guidance forces
\item Transfer and limitation of dynamic forces, e.g. due to
\begin{itemize}
		\item Track position errors
		\item Curves
		\item Switches
		\item Dynamic between vehicles
		\end{itemize} 
\item Effective attenuation of oscilation
\item Transfer of traction and braking forces
